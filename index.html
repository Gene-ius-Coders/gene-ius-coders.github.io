Services

Gene-ius-Coders

Gene-ius-Coder is a team of highly skilled professionals specializing in Bioinformatics and Genetics solutions, Medical Image Processing, and AI-based solutions. They studied bioinformatics at Tarbiat Modares University and have since gained extensive knowledge and experience in the field. With a focus on using computational methods to analyze biological data, the team is dedicated to providing innovative solutions to complex biological problems. Their expertise in Medical Image Processing allows them to develop new diagnostic tools, while their AI-based solutions automate many tasks involved in the life sciences industry. Gene-ius-Coder is committed to advancing the field of bioinformatics and genetics through cutting-edge technologies and research.

What We Offer

Structural Bioinformatics

Structural Bioinformatics uses computational methods to analyze and predict the structure and function of biological macromolecules

DNA Sequence Analysis

DNA sequence analysis involves identifying and interpreting genetic information computationally

RNA Sequence Analysis

RNA sequence analysis involves studying gene expression computationally from RNA data

QSAR Modeling

QSAR models are used to predict the biological activity of compounds based on their chemical structure

Medical Image Processing

Medical image processing combines advanced algorithms and computing to improve diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning

AI-based Solutions

AI in healthcare has the potential to improve diagnostics, personalized treatment, and patient outcomes
